Storage

PV and PVC

This lesson explains Persistent Volumes (PV) and Persistent Volume Claims (PVC) in Kubernetes.

Persistent Volume

  • A PV is cluster storage provisioned by an administrator or dynamically by a storage class.
  • It represents storage capacity, access mode, and reclaim behavior.

Persistent Volume Claim

  • A PVC is a request for storage by a pod.
  • It binds to a PV that matches storage size and access mode.
  • Pods use PVCs in their volumes section.
